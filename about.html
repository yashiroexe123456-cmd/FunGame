ℹ️ About Typing Racing Game

Improve your typing speed while racing against AI

🎮 Game Overview

Typing Racing Game combines racing excitement with real typing practice.

You control a racing car by typing sentences correctly and quickly. The faster and more accurate you type, the faster your car moves!

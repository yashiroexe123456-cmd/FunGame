🎮 How to Play Typing Racing

📝 Basic Rules

⌨️

Type the sentence shown above the input box correctly to make your car move forward

🚗

Your car (🚗) races against the computer car (🚙)

🎯

First car to reach the finish line wins!

📊 Level System

🟢 Easy Level

  • Short, simple sentences
  • Slow computer opponent
  • Perfect for beginners
  • More forgiving typing speed

🟠 Medium Level

  • Medium-length sentences
  • Faster computer opponent
  • Requires good typing accuracy
  • Balanced challenge

🔴 Hard Level

  • Long, complex sentences
  • Very fast computer opponent
  • Demands quick & accurate typing
  • For typing champions!

💡 Pro Tips

1

Start with Easy level to learn the mechanics

2

Focus on accuracy first, speed will come naturally

3

Use the Next Level button when you're ready for a challenge

4

Practice daily to improve your typing speed!

🎯 Scoring System

✅

Each correct character typed moves your car forward

🏆

Win a level to advance to the next difficulty

📊

Track your wins and losses at the top of the screen

⭐

Beat all three levels to become the Typing Champion!
